Our Story

A Coastal Retreat in the Heart of North Pattaya

Seven Zea Chic Hotel is a modern boutique hotel located in North Pattaya, just under 150 meters from Pattaya Beach. Designed for comfort and simplicity, it offers a relaxing stay near the sea while staying connected to the city's main attractions.

Our thoughtfully designed rooms, attentive service, and ideal beachside location make Seven Zea the perfect base for both leisure travelers and those seeking a calm coastal escape in Pattaya.
